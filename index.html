Why Zayvori

Everything your kitchen needs

Stop losing recipes to forgotten bookmarks and scattered notes. Zayvori brings order to your culinary world.

Save from Anywhere

Clip recipes from any website, social media, or type them in manually. If it's a recipe, Zayvori can store it.

Organize Your Way

Create custom collections — weeknight dinners, holiday baking, meal prep — and find any recipe in seconds.

Discover New Favorites

Browse curated collections and trending recipes from home cooks around the world. Your next favorite dish is waiting.

Smart Shopping Lists

Turn any recipe into a grocery list automatically. Scale servings up or down and never forget an ingredient again.

Share with Family

Share collections with family and friends. Keep the holiday cookie recipe in a shared collection everyone can access.

Cook Mode

Step-by-step cook mode keeps your screen awake and font large. Follow along without touching your phone with greasy hands.

Simple by Design

From discovery to dinner in three steps

01

Find a Recipe You Love

Browse Zayvori's collection, paste a link from your favorite food blog, or type in grandma's secret recipe. Any source works.

02

Save it to Your Collection

One tap saves it forever. Tag it, rate it, add notes, and drop it into the right collection so you can find it when you need it.

03

Cook with Confidence

Open Cook Mode for a clean, distraction-free view. Generate your shopping list and get to the kitchen — dinner is handled.

Why Zayvori

Everything you need, nothing you don't

No social pressure, no algorithm, no noise. Just a calm, personal space for the recipes you love.

Save from Anywhere

Clip recipes from any website, social media, or type them in manually. If it's a recipe, Zayvori can store it.

Organize Your Way

Create custom collections — weeknight dinners, holiday baking, meal prep — and find any recipe in seconds.

Discover the Community

Browse recipes shared freely by the Zayvori community. Search by keyword and heart the ones that inspire you — no pressure, no performance metrics.

Add Personal Notes

Jot down tweaks you made, substitutions that worked, or reminders for next time. Your notes live with the recipe, private to you.

Like What You Love

Heart anything from the community and find it instantly in your Liked tab. Your likes are private — nobody sees what you save or how many.

Offline Ready

No signal? No problem. Zayvori saves recipes locally so your collection is always accessible — in the kitchen, at the shops, or somewhere with patchy connection.

Our Philosophy

A shared table, open to everyone

Recipes are meant to be shared. All recipes in Zayvori are public — visible to everyone, contributed by anyone. Like sitting around one long table where everyone brings something they love.

No barriers. No judgement. Just food, shared freely.

Not a social network

There are no followers, no public profiles, no comment sections, and no visible like counts. No pressure to perform — just recipes to discover and enjoy.

No algorithm, no noise

Your collection is your own. Nobody sees your preferences, nobody judges your choices. No ranking, no competition, no feeling of being "not enough" — just cooking.

Simple by Design

From discovery to dinner in three steps

01

Find a Recipe You Love

Browse Zayvori's collection, paste a link from your favorite food blog, or type in grandma's secret recipe. Any source works.

02

Save it to Your Collection

One tap saves it forever. Add a note, drop it into the right collection, and find it instantly whenever you need it.

03

Cook, Then Make It Better

After you cook, add a personal note — what you changed, what worked. The recipe stays yours, improved by your own experience over time.
